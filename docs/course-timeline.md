# Course Timeline

This timeline is based on the course plan image and RA1 guide.

| Week | Date | Milestone |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Week 3 | May 19, 2026 | RA1 / first research assignment checkpoint |
| Week 7 | June 16, 2026 | Midway project presentation |
| Week 14 | August 4, 2026 | Project submission |
| Final period | August 9-18, 2026 | Final project presentation |

## Suggested Project Work Plan

Weeks 1-3:

- Finalize research focus.
- Choose cloud platform.
- Draft RA1 topic summary, problem statement, scope, research question, and team roles.
- Create initial architecture diagram.

Weeks 4-6:

- Build the simple three-tier application.
- Deploy the baseline cloud environment.
- Record insecure/default settings.

Week 7:

- Prepare midway presentation.
- Demonstrate the app and early baseline evidence.

Weeks 8-10:

- Implement identity, access, and secrets controls.
- Implement network exposure and perimeter controls.
- Re-run tests and collect after-state evidence.

Weeks 11-13:

- Improve experiments.
- Organize evidence.
- Write final analysis and limitations.

Week 14 and final period:

- Submit project.
- Present the practical demo and research findings.
